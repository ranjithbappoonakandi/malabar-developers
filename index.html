ABOUT

Construction Company started in the year 1972 by the Great Visionary, Mr. Cherian Varkey, who possesses Experience of 60 years in Construction, Management and Contracting.

Cherian Varkey Construction Company is dedicated to offering exemplary construction solutions for an extensive range of engineering projects. The company started off as a humble proprietary concern, is now a trendsetter in the infrastructure industry, engaged in providing customized, specialized and turnkey projects to the government, reputed private organisations and corporates. The company holds extensive experience in the construction of Foundations, Buildings, Roads, Railway lines, Marine bridges, Road over bridges, Railway bridges, Airports, Large factories and Commercial buildings with multiple basements, Power stations, Marine structures, Metro stations, Metro viaducts etc.

AREA OF EXPERTISE

From the point of inception, Cherian Varkey Construction Company is adding value and assets to the sphere of infrastructure development with our significant expertise and efficient workforce, supported by apt modern machinery for speedy execution of projects and profound knowledge for maintaining perfection in every construction element. The company is contributing best to the development of the nation by building the base and face of infrastructures that are constructed to remain as standing monuments for the future.
